Public Questions & Answers

Q: My salary is delayed 2 months. What should I do?
A: Keep payslips, written proof. File complaint at MOL portal or Musaned. (General guidance)
Q: Can I transfer sponsorship immediately after resignation?
A: Usually no — check contract and MOL rules. Special cases apply for unpaid wages. (General guidance)
